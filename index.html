About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Center for Geospatial Analytics (CGA) at North Carolina State University, Raleigh, NC, USA. My main interests lie in interactive visualizations of multi-dimensional geospatial data using open source technologies and integrating dynamic geospatial modeling with tangible user interfaces. As a member of the NCSU GeoForAll Laboratory I promote using open source technologies in science and education and develop new open source tools for dynamic spatio-temporal data visualization. Particularly, I am an active member of GRASS Development team, developing and maintaining GRASS GIS, an open source GIS.
